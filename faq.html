Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

How do I create a quiz?

To create a quiz, click on the "Quiz" section in the header, select your preferred quiz type, and follow the step-by-step instructions.

Can I use Quizizz for my classroom?

Yes! Quizizz is designed for both personal and classroom use. Teachers can create and share quizzes with students easily.

Do I need an account to play?

Creating an account is optional for students playing quizzes. However, teachers and creators need an account to manage and track quizzes.

Is Quizizz free?

Yes! Quizizz offers a free plan with essential features. Additional premium features are available with paid plans.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team via the "Contact Us" link in the header or email us at support@quizizz.com.
